Game Characteristics

Essence of the Game

Users can earn tokens by clicking on an image, which will give them 1000 tokens per click. However, every 24 hours they can click 2000 times, allowing them to earn 2,000,000 SCRATS tokens.

Referral System

When a new user joins, every existing user will receive a reward of 100 SCRATS tokens.

Subscriptions

Users can subscribe to our Telegram chat and x.com (Twitter), and for each subscription, they will receive an additional 50,000 SCRATS tokens. In the future, we will integrate a boost function that will multiply the rewards for clicks. Stay tuned for updates!

Invite Friends and Develop the Project

Invite your friends, develop the project, and you will be rewarded!
